      Google Operators

      Examples of using Google to search real names:

      “John Doe” “Chicago”

      “John Doe” “Microsoft” 

      Site:instagram.com “John Doe”

      Site:twitter.com “John Doe” AND “city” OR “sports team” (see next specific example)

      site:twitter.com "James Davidson" AND broncos

      Unique names will require fewer operators  to filter out false positives.
