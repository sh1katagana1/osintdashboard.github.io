    Sh1katagana1 OSINT Dashboard  

    Updated: September 17, 2024

     This dashboard was created as a tool for myself to use for general OSINT and investigations.

    Each entry is self explanatory with prompts telling you what type of input it expects, and some have a button to type in the input and hit Populate All to fill out the remaining fields with that input.

    Be aware that sites come and go and some links that were active at one time, may not be active at a later date. These can be periodically reviewed and removed as needed by myself.

    Usage Notes:

    1. Several tools require browser popup authorization, especially the "Submit All" features.
    2. Some tools are currently non-functional, but they remain here because many services come back to life after a short hiatus.
    3. We do not see, collect, or store the information you search within these tools. The queries are generated and executed within your own browser.

    Workflow Recommendations

    1. Opsec - understand your connection, workstation, VMs, accounts, and compartmentalize to match your threat level
    2. Workspace: Digital notebook, paper scratch pad, case directory, browser with extensions and FB, Twitter, and IG accounts
    3. Define and/or clarify the question you are working to answer, add this to your notes
    4. Premium, proprietary, and/or gov/LE checks (DOL, booking photos, etc.)
    5. Use these tools to query the most unique identifiers (email addresses, unique names or usernames, domains, etc.)
    6. Remember to unblock pop-ups the first time you use each category of the tools
    7. Use OneTab or another extension to manager your tabs
    8. Work through the results pulling any minor leads into a new tab and any strong leads into their own window
    9. Any strong leads should also be added to your digital or analogue notes (ie: new email, phone number, account, address, etc)
    10. Take 30-seconds up front to make a plan and then re-assess periodically
