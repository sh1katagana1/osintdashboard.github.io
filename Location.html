    Additional Resources

    Google Mapping and Imagery Resources: 

    https://earth.google.com/web/

    Streetview and basic functions are lower right on the UI

    Menu -> Settings (Change Lat/Long format as necessary)

    If you enable “Photos” you may get some geotagged photo results on the map

    Mapstyle -> Custom (adjust labels)

    Choose “Add Placemark” -> Edit Place (This brings up Lat/Long and a capture button)

    https://www.google.com/earth/studio/ | Sign up with a burner account, good for preparing high value briefings or post incident reviews, visuals for court

    https://earthengine.google.com/timelapse/ | Showing long term changes in rural areas such as development or construction of large facilities

    Microsoft/Bing Mapping and Imagery Resources: https://www.bing.com/maps

    An address search vs manual zooming will bring up additional Bing data such as Zillow photos of the address. 

    Right-click will bring up the Lat-Long for easy copy as well as a “birds-eye” option if available. The image source will be listed on the lower right.

    Streetview is indicated with blue lines and will have the capture date top right. It tends to be less current than Google Streetview.

    https://spaghettimodels.com/default.htm | Weather related satalite imagery some with meterological and storm models included
